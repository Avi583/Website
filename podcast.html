Liontalk


Liontalk was a podcast that I host that covers both political and philosophical issues. I started Liontalk in my sophomore year at Penn State and recorded a total of 17 episodes (along with a special 18th episode in collaboration with Penn State College Independents). The podcast ran a little over a year from February 25th, 2019 to March 29th 2020. Since I am no longer a Penn State student Liontalk is officially over.

Liontalk Episode 14:

Description:

In episode 14, we try to define the idea of "utopia" and why the term is potentially problematic to define. We discuss the idea of post-scarcity and the meaning of scarcity and how disastrous it can be for society. We also touch upon the ideas of death and how this cycle is important for society. Finally, we wrap up the discussion by talking about virtual reality (VR)/augmented reality and what that could mean for the idea of a utopia.


Liontalk Episode 13:

Description:

Episode 13 is a longer topic. We were not able to cover everything in detail but we tried to cover a few basic ideas. We also discuss viewpoints of nationalism vs. globalism through the lens of doing what is best for your country (and citizens) versus what is best for the world (and everyone as a whole).
